Our Core Service

A complete system tear-off, never an overlay.

When your roof reaches the end of its lifespan, patching layers over damaged shingles only traps moisture and accelerates structural decay. At Vincenzo's Roofing, we perform a complete tear-off straight down to the wooden decking.

We inspect every sheet of plywood, replace rotted timber, and build a multi-layer protective barrier—including heavy-duty ice-and-water shields engineered specifically for New Hampshire's freeze-and-thaw cycles.

  • 100% complete tear-off to the roof deck
  • Ice-and-water shield along all eaves, valleys, & penetration points
  • Architectural shingles with 130 mph wind resistance ratings
  • Magnetic sweep cleanup to catch all nails and debris
Our Step-by-Step Method

How we replace your roof in 4 smooth steps.

Step 01

Deck Inspection

We strip old shingles, clean the roof deck, and replace any rotted or sagging plywood sheets.

Step 02

Ice & Water Shield

Heavy synthetic underlayment and self-sealing ice shields are laid down across all eaves and valleys.

Step 03

Precision Shingling

Architectural shingles are installed using stainless steel fasteners and factory-calibrated nail lines.

Step 04

Final Walkthrough

We clean gutters, run magnetic land sweeps, and hand over your signed 25-year warranty document.
