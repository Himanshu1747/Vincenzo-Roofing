FAQ

Straight answers first.

Most full roof replacements in New Hampshire run between $8,000 and $22,000, depending on roof size, pitch, material, and how much of the underlying deck needs repair. We provide a free, itemized estimate after an on-site inspection so you know the exact cost before any work begins.

Most NH homeowners policies cover sudden storm, wind, and hail damage, though coverage details vary by carrier. We document the damage, meet with your adjuster, and help you understand what your policy covers as part of every storm damage claim.

Most residential roof replacements are completed in one to three days, weather permitting. Larger or steeper roofs, and commercial flat roofs, can take longer. You'll get a specific timeline in your written estimate.
